Explore some hotels of Himachal

Anandam Homestay

Dharamshala, the home of His Holiness Dalai Lama, attracts a big chunk of visitors throughout the year. Located here is my homestay which is a perfect place for all the budget travellers, solo travellers and all the ones who are more of a traveller and less of a tourist. The homestay has nicely carpeted floors with a simple and home like dcor which will make you comfortable instantly. There is a lot for you to do here, like read books, go out to explore the local markets or simply laze around here all day long.

Hotel Imperial 9 Mcleodganj

Located on a spur of Dhauladhar range, land known for its natural beauty and religious calmness, Dharamshala, Hotel Imperial 9 is an ideal getaway offering comfortableness and affordability at its best. It is an ideal spot for holidaymakers who wish to spend some quality time away from the hustle and bustle of city life. The property offers a fine blend of recreation and relief at it's best.

Hotel Grand Riviera

Hotel Grand Riviera is a budget property, located away from hustle-bustle of the city. It is just 5.5 kms from Paonta Sahib Bus Stop. The property offers facilities like doctor on call, room service, laundry service, power backup, travel desk and parking. The warm and beautifully crafted rooms provide amenities like television, telephone and attached bathroom with running hot/cold water. The hotel is nearby to some prominent landmarks like Gurudwara Shri Paonta Sahib (5 kms), Yamuna Mandir (5.1 kms), Vishwakarma Mandir (5.3 kms), Govindghat (5.6 kms) and Gurudwara Shri Kirpal Shilla (5.8 kms).

Hotel Combermere

With customisable services backed by traditional Indian hospitality, Hotel Combermere features incredible dining options and trendy accommodation options. Features Ayurvedic massages and other wellness treatments at the in-house spa. Features rooms with a bay window offering a magnificent view of Shimla's valleys. Presents a Game Zone decked with games such a pool table and table tennis table. Serves delicious multi cuisines, with a speciality of Thai and Mexican cuisines at the on-site restaurant.
