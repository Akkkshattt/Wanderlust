Hotel Ariya Nivaas

Celebrated as one of the city's best is, Ariya Nivas Hotel in Thiruvananthapuram. The hotel is strategically located in Thampanoor. This hospitality destination has grown to become the ideal place for travelers and those on business to feel at home when in the city. Many have also reviewed the hotel to be among the sought after Hotels in Thiruvananthapuram. The hotel functions from 00:00 - 23:59 all through the week.

Vivanta Thiruvananthapuram

Vivanta Thiruvananthapuram is located in the centre of city in close proximity to major government offices and commercial hubs. With just a 20 minutes drive from Trivandrum International Airport, the hotel offer 108 brand new state of the art of rooms. There are one specialty restaurant (Kerala Cuisine) and All Day Dining and a Cafe. The hotel is also just 30 minutes drive to Technopark where most of the Major IT companies have their presence.

The Residency Tower

Deluxe & Elegant rooms set in a modern building at this 4-star hotel located in god's own country, Kerala. The property features well-appointed and comfortable rooms apt for different types of guests. There's also a vibrant bar, two restaurants, a coffee shop, an outdoor pool and a fitness room.

Hotel Aiswarya

Situated near the beautiful destination of Marine Drive Kochi, Hotel Aiswarya offers the luxuries of an in-house restaurant and exclusive services. The hotel is easily accessible from Ernakulam Junction (850 m). Guests can enjoy delicious food and complimentary breakfast at the in-house restaurant. Popular tourist sites like the Murugan Temple (700 m) are within proximity of the hotel.
